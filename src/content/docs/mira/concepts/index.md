## What's in this section

- **[The GTM kit](/mira/concepts/the-gtm-kit/)** — the six artifact sections produced for each catalog entry.
- **[Versioning & approval](/mira/concepts/versioning-and-approval/)** — the one-way state machine and why published assets pin to frozen versions.
- **[Agents](/mira/concepts/agents/)** — the three specialized agents (generation, outbound, learning) and their shared trust contract.
- **[Multi-tenancy & isolation](/mira/concepts/multi-tenancy-and-isolation/)** — how tenants stay out of each other's data.
- **[How AI generation is sandboxed](/mira/concepts/how-ai-generation-is-sandboxed/)** — the two boundaries that contain the blast radius of bad LLM output.
