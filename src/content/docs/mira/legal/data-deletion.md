This page explains how to request deletion of personal data and Instagram-connected data held by Mira.

## How to request deletion

Email **privacy@hereai.com** with the subject line **Data deletion request**.

Include enough information for us to locate and verify the relevant account:

- Your name
- Your email address used with Mira
- Your organization or workspace name, if applicable
- Your Instagram username, Instagram professional account name, or Facebook Page name, if your request concerns Instagram-connected data
- A short statement that you want your data deleted

Do not send passwords, access tokens, payment card numbers, government IDs, or other sensitive secrets in the email.

## Verification

We may ask you to verify control of the Mira account, workspace, email address, or Instagram professional account connected to the request. We use verification only to confirm that the request is legitimate and to prevent unauthorized deletion of another person's or organization's data.

## What we delete

After verification, we will delete or anonymize personal data associated with your request where we are able and legally permitted to do so. For Instagram-connected data, this can include:

- Instagram OAuth tokens and channel credentials stored for Mira
- Instagram business account IDs, usernames, and Page/account connection metadata
- Audience-member identity links created from Instagram IDs
- Instagram direct message content and message metadata stored in Mira
- Generated or drafted replies tied to the Instagram conversation
- Conversation logs and operational records tied to the requesting user or account, where deletion does not break required audit, security, or contractual records

For Mira workspace data not tied to Instagram, deletion can include account profile data, captured lead data, uploaded files, generated drafts, and related records tied to the verified request.

## What may be retained

Some information may be retained when deletion is not technically or legally appropriate, including:

- Security logs needed to protect the service or investigate abuse
- Billing, tax, accounting, or contract records
- Audit records required to show who approved, sent, changed, or deleted business records
- Records that another customer, workspace owner, or controller is legally responsible for handling
- Aggregated, de-identified, or anonymized analytics that no longer identify a person
- Backup copies until they expire through the normal backup lifecycle

Where we retain information, we will explain the reason when responding to the request.

## Timing

We aim to acknowledge deletion requests within **10 business days** and complete verified requests within **30 days**, unless the request is unusually complex, legally restricted, or requires action by a customer workspace owner or another controller. If more time is needed, we will provide an update.

## Removing Mira from Meta settings

You can also remove Mira from your Meta account settings:

1. Open Facebook or Meta account settings.
2. Go to **Settings & Privacy -> Settings -> Apps and Websites**.
3. Find Mira or the relevant HereAI app.
4. Remove the app.
5. If Meta offers the option, select **Send Request** to request deletion of data shared with the app.

Removing the app stops future access through that Meta authorization. To delete data already stored by Mira, send the email request described above.

## Status updates

When we receive a verified request, we will respond by email with the current status and any next steps. If a confirmation code is needed for a platform request, we will include it in the response.
