This section collects public-facing policy pages for Mira. These pages are written as publication-ready sample copy and should be reviewed by counsel before they are used as binding legal terms.

## Platform review URLs

Use these public URLs in platform dashboards such as Meta App Review:

| Platform field | URL |
| --- | --- |
| Privacy Policy URL | `https://docs.hereai.com/mira/legal/privacy-policy/` |
| Terms of Service URL | `https://docs.hereai.com/mira/legal/terms-of-service/` |
| User Data Deletion instructions URL | `https://docs.hereai.com/mira/legal/data-deletion/` |

For Meta App Review, the **User Data Deletion instructions URL** option is the lowest-operational-burden path. Use a callback URL only if the app is ready to receive Meta's signed deletion request, create a deletion request record, and return a status URL plus confirmation code.

## Pages

- **[Privacy Policy](/mira/legal/privacy-policy/)** - what data Mira collects, how it is used, how Instagram-connected data is handled, and how users can exercise privacy rights.
- **[Terms of Service](/mira/legal/terms-of-service/)** - baseline service terms for Mira users, including account responsibilities, AI output review, third-party services, and acceptable use.
- **[Data deletion instructions](/mira/legal/data-deletion/)** - public instructions for deleting Mira account data and Instagram-connected data.

## Notes for review

Before publishing or submitting these URLs, confirm:

- The contact inboxes listed on the pages are monitored.
- The company legal name, governing law, and contract hierarchy are correct.
- The retention timelines match production settings and customer contracts.
- The subprocessors page is current.
- Instagram/Meta scopes requested by the app match the data described in the privacy policy.
