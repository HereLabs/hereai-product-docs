Mira is HereAI's catalog-native GTM engine for multi-product B2B SaaS companies. It ingests a product catalog and gives each entry its own go-to-market motion: positioning, landing pages, outbound assets, lead capture, routing, and learning.

> Mira is **not** a CRM. It writes context into one.

## Why Mira

Most B2B SaaS companies have 20–200 marketable products, modules, integrations, vertical packages, and add-ons — but only one or two product marketers. The flagship gets a real GTM motion; everything else makes do with a generic page and a flagship-flavored email. Mira closes that gap.

What teams use Mira for:

- **Launches that ship with publish-ready pages.** A new module gets its own ICP, positioning, page, and capture form on day one — not when there's PMM headcount.
- **Sales handoff with full product context.** Every captured lead reaches your CRM with the product the prospect cared about, the approved positioning excerpt, and a deep link back to the workspace.
- **Outcome-aware iteration.** Visits, form fills, replies, meetings, and CRM stages flow back to the [learning agent](/mira/concepts/agents/#learning-agent), which proposes narrative changes for the next pass.
