Administration controls who can do what in Mira and provides an immutable record of every state-changing action.

## What's in this section

- **[Roles & permissions](/mira/administration/roles-and-permissions/)** — the three default roles (Admin, Editor, Viewer), authentication options, and how multi-approver workflows are configured.
- **[Audit log](/mira/administration/audit-log/)** — every approval, publish, configuration change, and user event. 12 months immutable.
- **[Billing & cost reporting](/mira/administration/billing-and-cost-reporting/)** — per-tenant cost dashboard, plan limits, and threshold alerts.
